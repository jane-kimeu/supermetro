Who We Are

Super metro is One of the leading and biggest matatu Sacco in Nairobi. It operates Nairobi Kikuyu , Nairobi juja, Nairobi Thika and Nairobi Makongeni routes. ... Matatu industry in Kenya has always had a notorious reputation for being rowdy and unreasonable in their fares.Most resident's always complain especially in rainy seasons when Matatu cartels hike fares to almost double the usual rates. The touts and other orderlies of Matatus are always rude to customers who question the fares Super metro have taken advantage of this gap and employed smart and polite crew.

Their fare charges are well structured and they do not change according to weather patterns or time of day. The Sacco has brought order to an industry that has been thriving under confusion.
